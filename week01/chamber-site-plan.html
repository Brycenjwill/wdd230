Kennett Square Chamber Site Plan

Site Name: Kennett Square Chamber of commerce. This name represents the city of Kennett Square, where the chamber is located.

Description: "The Kennett Square Chamber of Commerce is committed to serving the region as the leading force for our business community. The Chamber promotes trade, commerce, industry, and sustainable economic development."

Typography: Arial will be used for the entirety of the website. This is because this site will be for government purposes and arial is one of the few court-approved legal document fonts for readability.

Color scheme: My color scheme will be mostly greyed-greens, to fit the colors of the area without being too strong. The color #52796F will be used as the background for most main sections, with white as the main text color for those sections. #354F52 will be used for headers font, and for the background of navigation bars/menus. #2F3E46 is for the background of footers, with #CAD2C5 as the footer font color and a backup font color for where white doesn't fit. #2F3E46 is generally used where black font would not fit well.

Wireframes: Desktop on the left. Mobile on right.
